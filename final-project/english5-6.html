English 5-6 Course Syllabus

This course is a study in American Literature, from colonization to modern times. Throughout this course, we will explore seminal government documents, primary sources, poetry, drama, and novels by American authors. Our curriculum is designed in alignment with the Nebraska State English Language Arts Standards and selections are taken from our HMH Collections textbook and selected novels. Novel selections will include:

  • The Absolutely True Diary of a Part-Time Indian
  • The Glass Castle
  • The Great Gatsby
  • To Kill a Mockingbird

During each class period, students should expect to read, write, speak, and listen. Academic conversations with peers will occur regularly, and students will be exptected to engage with peers regarding academic content.
