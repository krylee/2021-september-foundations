Curriculum

Our district has adopted HMH Collections textbooks as our primary English Language Arts curriculum. Collections contains a balanced combination of short stories, poetry, drama selections, nonfiction essays, and primary source documents. These selections allow students to learn the essential skills outlined in the Nebraska State English Language Arts Standards. They also prepare students for standardized assessments, such as the ACT, which is required for all eleventh grade students in the state of Nebraska.

Grading Policies

Our school district has adopted standards-based grading, meaning that students receive grades based on their ability to meet curriculum standards rather than percentages. Standards-based grading measures proficiency in using specific academic skills.
