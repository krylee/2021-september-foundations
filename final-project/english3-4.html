English 3-4 Course Syllabus

This course is a study in literature and non-fiction collections covering the themes of identity, change, justice, and power. Our curriculum is designed in alignment with the Nebraska State English Language Arts Standards and selections are taken from our HMH Collections textbook and selected novels. Reading selections will include:

  • The Freedom Writers Diary
  • The Five People You Meet in Heaven
  • American Born Chinese
  • Macbeth

During each class period, students should expect to read, write, speak, and listen. Academic conversations with peers will occur regularly, and students will be exptected to engage with peers regarding academic content.
