Bio

Ms. Rylee lives in Omaha with her daughter, Lily, and her dog, Minnie. She loves to read, re-watch episodes of Scrubs and The Simpsons, attend concerts, and try new restaurants. Before becoming a teacher, Ms. Rylee worked for many years as a claims examiner. She began teaching for Omaha Public Schools in 2017 and has been teaching for almost 5 years.

Education

Ms. Rylee attended Metropolitan Community College, where she earned an Associate of Arts degree in Liberal arts. She then transferred to Creighton University where she earned a Bachelor of Arts in English. Just a few months after finishing her bachelor's degree, Ms. Rylee decided she wanted to teach, so she attended College of Saint Mary, where she earned a Master of Arts in Teaching. Most recently, Ms. Rylee earned a Master of Science in Special Education at the University of Nebraska Omaha.
