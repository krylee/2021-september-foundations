I want something about the apocalypse!

You might enjoy World War Z!

This novel is a compilation of interviews after a virus turned hoardes of humans into zombies, resulting in a worldwide zombie war. The main character is a reporter who catalogues accounts of people from many different walks of life who each have unique perspectives on the zombie war, from rock stars to high-ranking military officials. An audio version is also available and read by a full cast.
